ScaleX Personal Assistant is a suite of **pre-built autonomous trading agents** deployed directly on ScaleX DEX. Each agent handles a specific trading strategy, executes within user-defined policy limits, and operates fully on-chain no manual intervention required.

Every agent has a character. A personality forged from real-world archetypes that embody the strategy they execute. They don't just trade they *think* the way their persona would.

---

## The Agents

### 1. ScaleX DCA Bot

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ScaleX DCA Bot.jpg" alt="ScaleX DCA Bot" />
</Frame>

**Strategy:** Periodic market buys on a schedule. Set-and-forget dollar-cost averaging across supported trading pairs on ScaleX DEX.

**Persona:** The Japanese salary worker. Every payday, without fail, a portion goes into the market. No emotion, no hesitation just disciplined, punctual execution. Rain or shine, bull or bear, the DCA Bot shows up and puts capital to work on schedule.

> *"Consistency beats timing. Every. Single. Time."*

### 3. Dip Buyer

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Dip Buyer.jpg" alt="Dip Buyer" />
</Frame>

**Strategy:** Places limit buy orders at configurable percentages below current market price. Catches pullbacks automatically.

**Persona:** The pawn shop owner. Sharp-eyed, patient, and always hunting for things priced below their true worth. He never pays full price ever. The moment something hits his number, the deal is done. No sentimentality, no second-guessing. Just value.

> *"You want full price? Go somewhere else. I'll wait."*

### 4. Lending Optimizer

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Lending Optimizer.jpg" alt="Lending Optimizer" />
</Frame>

**Strategy:** Auto-supplies idle tokens to lending pools for yield. Monitors health factors and rebalances positions.

**Persona:** The old-school banker. Conservative, methodical, and deeply uncomfortable with idle capital. In his world, money sitting still is money being wasted. He routes every unused token into yield-generating positions and watches the health factor the way a banker watches a credit score.

> *"Idle capital is a sin. Put it to work."*

### 5. Range Trader

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Range Trader.jpg" alt="Range Trader" />

**Persona:** The beach tourist. Completely unbothered. He set his towel between support and resistance, ordered a drink, and let the market do the work. He's not chasing pumps or panicking at dips — he's just relaxing within his range collecting profits as the price bounces back and forth.
