During the building period, we accomplished the following:

# Frontend Development

Built a user-friendly interface for **Lending Protocol**, **Spot Yield Bearing Token Trading**, and **Faucet** functionality across desktop and mobile platforms.

# Smart Contracts

Designed and deployed smart contracts covering core protocol functionalities including lending, spot trading, and yield-bearing token mechanics, across multiple testnet environments to validate security, performance, and composability ahead of mainnet.

# Privy Integration

Integrated Privy to provide a seamless and secure authentication experience. Users can sign in using their email or social accounts, removing the friction of traditional wallet setups and making ScaleX accessible to both crypto-native and mainstream users.

# Testing and Deployment

Conducted rigorous testing on PC and mobile to ensure seamless operations and deployed the project with all features integrated.

# ScaleX Agent

Launched the **ScaleX Agent** an autonomous AI agent that monitors on-chain data and market conditions, surfaces actionable trading signals, and executes trades on your behalf. This marks the first step toward a fully agentic trading experience where your portfolio works for you around the clock.
