During the building period, we set out to solve one core problem: **capital in DeFi is chronically underperforming**. Users choose between trading and earning. Agents act without guardrails. Liquidation wipes out positions that shouldn't be liquidated. We built ScaleX to eliminate all of that.

Here's what we accomplished.

# Frontend Development

Built a unified interface for **Lending Protocol**, **Spot Yield-Bearing Token Trading**, and **Faucet** functionality across desktop and mobile platforms — giving both human users and agents a single surface to deposit, trade, bet, and earn without switching protocols.

# Smart Contracts

Designed and deployed smart contracts covering the full capital stack: lending, spot trading, yield-bearing token mechanics, and auto-borrow / auto-repay logic. Validated across multiple testnet environments to confirm security, performance, and composability ahead of mainnet.

# Privy Integration

Integrated Privy to provide seamless, secure authentication. Users sign in with email or social accounts — removing wallet friction and making ScaleX accessible to both crypto-native users and mainstream audiences who want capital efficiency without complexity.

# Testing and Deployment

Conducted rigorous testing across PC and mobile to ensure every capital flow — deposit, yield accrual, order placement, borrowing, repayment, and liquidation — works seamlessly end-to-end. Deployed with all features integrated and live on testnet.

# ScaleX Agent

Launched the **ScaleX Agent** — an optional automation layer that executes trading and yield strategies on your behalf, within rules you define. The agent operates on-chain with full policy enforcement: no key sharing, no unchecked execution, no black-box behavior.

For users who want to automate, the agent turns ScaleX's capital efficiency into a hands-free system — trading, borrowing, repaying, and earning around the clock while every idle dollar continues generating yield.
