## Quick summary

ScaleX is the **Most Capital Efficient and Safe DApp for Agent and Human** — a unified platform where anyone can trade or bet while automatically earning yield on every deposited dollar. Powered by a hybrid CLOB + lending engine, ScaleX eliminates idle capital, protects against liquidation, and manages borrowing costs — all while staying fully self-custodial and DeFi-composable. For those who want to automate, a built-in Agent layer executes strategies on-chain within user-defined policy.
