## Quick summary


Our product offers a hybrid CLOB + lending experience that delivers: higher capital efficiency, lower liquidation and borrowing costs, built-in yield generation, and dual revenue streams — while preserving self-custody and DeFi composability.


---


## Compared to: Standalone Order Book DEX (dYdX, Vertex)


- ✅ **Built-in yield generation** on idle trading capital
- ✅ **Zero-slippage liquidations** via order book protection
- ✅ **Smart loan repayment automation**
- ✅ **4x higher capital efficiency**
- ✅ **Dual revenue streams** vs single trading fees

Traders and lenders want **efficient capital use**, **transparent pricing and custody**, and **independent vault**.
