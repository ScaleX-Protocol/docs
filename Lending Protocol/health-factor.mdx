## Overview

The **Health Factor (HF)** represents the overall safety of a user's borrowing position.  
It measures how close an account is to liquidation  a higher value means a safer position.

It's a powerful way to examine risk in a specific collateral on debt, but it doesn't fit when the user has multiple collateral assets or debts.

<Info>
Health Factor like a credit score but works for onchain DeFi. Higher means further from liquidation.
</Info>

---

## Formula

Health Factor = Risk-adjusted Collateral Value / Risk-adjusted Debt Value

Each asset has its own **risk weight**, reflecting the volatility.  
For example, stablecoins typically have higher collateral factors than volatile tokens like ETH or BTC.

<Tip>
When our Health Factor drops below **1.0**, our position becomes eligible for liquidation. So, maintaining a buffer above 1.5 helps protect collateral from sudden market swings.
</Tip>

---

## Health Factor Safety Levels

| Health Factor Range | Status | Description |
|----------------------|---------|--------------|
| **Above 2.0** | 🟢 Very Safe | plenty of buffer |
| **1.5 – 2.0** | 🟡 Safe | Healthy position, golden spot |
| **1.0 – 1.5** | 🟠 Moderate | Monitor closely  nearing liquidation threshold |
| **Below 1.0** | 🔴 Liquidation Triggered | Collateral is triggerd |

---

## Shortcut

The Health Factor sensitive with market prices:

- **Collateral value increases** or **Repay debt →** HF increasing made position become safer  
- **Collateral value decreases** or **Borrow more →** HF falling made position become riskier
