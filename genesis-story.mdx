A trader wants to buy 1 BTC at \$60,000 - what they believe is the perfect price level. With \$60,000 capital, they face a **tough choice**:

- Place a limit order at \$60,000 and earn zero yield while waiting
- Supply \$60,000 to AAVE at 12% APY and earn \$600 monthly but risk missing BTC at \$60,000 if it pumps

If BTC jumps to \$65,000, they completely miss the trading opportunity. If they choose yield farming, they might miss the perfect entry at \$60,000.

**Worst case: they miss both the BTC trade AND the yield - leaving their capital completely unproductive.**

Most traders end up splitting their capital, compromising both their trading strategy and yield potential.

## The Two Walls Every Trader Hits

This isn't just bad luck. It's the result of two compounding problems that no one has solved until now.

**Wall 1: Human Limitation**

Markets don't sleep but humans do. **Emotion, fatigue, and divided attention** are not edge cases. They are the default state of every human trader. A perfect entry gets missed at 3AM. A stop-loss gets held too long because panic sets in. No matter how good your strategy is, your **biology works against you**.

**Wall 2: The Ecosystem Doesn't Allow It Yet**

Even with perfect discipline, today's crypto infrastructure forces a binary choice. Your capital is either locked in an order or earning yield, never both. Assets in **limit orders generate zero return**, and yield protocols have no awareness of your trading positions.

## The Scale of What We're Losing

These two walls together create a massive, silent drain on the entire market.
